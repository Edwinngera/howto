Persona
-----------------------------
Who
	- Basic -> Age, gender, Location, Family
	- Professional -> Job Title, Industry, Company size, Company Revenue
What
	- Problems - primary / secondary   problem
	- Goals - Primary / secondary goal
	- Values - Primary expectation, secondary Objection
How
	- Product Benefit - how will the person be better after using the produkt
	- Marketing - how will we explain the prod. to persona?
	- Marketing - how will we aquire customers that fit this persona?

Customer Persona Params
- Geographical - country, city, density, climate, population, continent
- Demographic - age, gender, family size, occupation, income, edication, religion, race, nationality
- Psychology - lifestyle, social class, AIOs, personal values, attitudes
- Behavioral - ocasions, degree loyalty, benefits sought, usage, user status, buyer readlines stage
